☕ Bean & Beat Nights
Special evenings with retro bands or open mic every Friday (7 PM -10 PM).
☕ Coffee & Vintage Market Day
Once a month, partner with local sellers who bring retro/vintage goods (vinyls, clothes, crafts). Turns the shop into a little retro bazaar. Don’t miss it out by checking our socials!
☕ Coffee & Crafts Corner
A DIY station available every weekends where customers can try retro crafts (friendship bracelets, tie-dye napkins, doodle art). Great for students and creative souls.
☕ Retro Photobooth Fun
Packed with vintage props, funky backdrops, and that nostalgic Polaroid feel. Experience it now!
☕ Typewriter Poetry Café
A vintage typewriter is available in the coffee shop. Customers can type short poems, love notes, or inspirational messages. Pin them on the “Retro Bean Wall of Words” for others to read.
